Instructors

Each of our instructors have been meditating for years. Let our experienced teachers be your guide on the exciting journey of exploring yoga and meditation. Each instructor has specialized in a particular field of yoga and is prepared to answer any question you have and teach on your level.

What is Yoga

Yoga is a relaxing way to exercise. It helps to balnce your mind, body, and soul. It is completely secular and belief in a higher being is not needed.

What are the fees?

We used a sliding scale fee. You tell us what you are able to afford and we work with that. We feel that everyone should experience meditation and we want to facilitate that. Donations are always accepted.

Are lessons consecutive?

All of our classes increase in ability and we encourage you to go through the program like that. However, if you feel like a class is holding you back from your potential, then go ahead and register for a higher level class. Our guided meditations can be taken in any order, though we do recommend listening to the introductory meditation either in person or downloaded from our site.

Why learn meditation?

Meditation allows us to connect with our emotions and understand what makes us human. Through meditation we can come to accept the things we cannot change. It is both humbling and empowering.
